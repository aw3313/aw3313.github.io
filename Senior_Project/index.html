Week 1: Responses

  • The Official Watchmen Podcast, Masks 1-3 Episode

    • I have been studying simulations lately with game design and such so when Lindelof talked about trying to make this comic book real and authentic as possible, this stood out to me for sure. His objective in his show is about “RACE”. I believe he makes it as obvious as possible, that is something that resonated with me since my creations are always about social issues. I should make it a priority to be very obvious with where my audience is at. I do feel as though Lindelof is teaching his own people about the “Race Problem” in America.


    3 inputs for Lindelof in creating watchmen

    1. Wanted to make it real and authentic as possible

    2. He was able to educate himself when working with watchmen about “Reparations” and “Black Wall-Street, Tulsa, Oklahoma”

    3. He discovered stuff through interpretations from other people, not only from the books itself.


    at least 3 inputs for your senior project

    1. When naming my creation, a great way would be to list of adjectives. (To describe the original Watchmen that’s how they created the name)

    2. Be obvious with what your creation is directed to, for example “RACE”

    3. My project will definitely be about education others who may not know or need to see the sickness and heal the cultural anxiety in America.
